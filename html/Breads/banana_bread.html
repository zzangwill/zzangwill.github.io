Banana Bread
Recipe from: Mother
Serves: 6
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 egg or 1/3 egg sub
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3/4 cup grated zucc
  • Chocolate chips

Mix the oil, sugar, vanilla, and eggs.

Smash the banana in a seperate bowl and add to the mixture.

Mix flour, baking soda, and salt seperately.

Add dry ingredients to wet, plus zucchini and chocolate chips.

Bake at 350 degrees for around 45 minutes.
