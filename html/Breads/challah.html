Challah
Recipe from: Aunt Linda
Makes: 2 large loaves
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 cup heated milk
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 2 eggs (1 whole, 1 white)
  • 2 pkg yeast
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 4-5 cup bread flour
  • 1 tsp salt

Pour warm water over yeast with sugar. Let sit.

Heat warm milk 1 min in microwabe (don't let boil), add oil and honey. Add eggs, and mix well.

Pour milk into large steel bowl with flour, salt, and yeast. Knead 10-15 min, adding minimal flour.

Line bowl with oil and place dough covered with damp towel until doubles. Punch down, braid, and let rise again.

Brush with egg yolk and any toppings. Bake 35-40 min if large, 28 min if small at 360. Should sound hollow when bottom is tapped!
