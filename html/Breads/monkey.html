Monkey Bread
Recipe from: Stefan
Serves: ~6
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 basic sweet dough recipe
  • 8 tbs unsalted butter, melt

    • Glaze:

  • 1 cup pow sugar
  • 2 tbs milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla

Grease a bundt pan. Toss brown sugar with cinnamon in a bowl or sheet. Roll sweet dough into balls (golf sized) and roll into melted butter, then sugar mix. Place into bundt pan. If desired, add chocolate chips in between the balls.

Wrap tightly with plastic and let rise until doubled, 1-1.5 hours.

With rack in the middle position, heat oven to 350. Bake bread until top is deep borwn and caramel bubbles on the edges, 30-40 minutes. Turn halfway through.

Cool only 5 minutes, then turn out. Cool another 5 minutes, whisk glaze and pour over.

To make ahead, don't let the dough rise. Instead refrigerate up to 16 hours. To use, let sit at room temperature until doubled, 1 hour, then continue.
