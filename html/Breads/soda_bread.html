Brown Soda Bread
Recipe from: Ireland
Serves: ~8
  • 675g whole wheat flour
  • 450g white bread flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp salt (or less)
  • 750 mL buttermilk (maybe 75mL more)

Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and gradually add buttermilk, taking flour from the sides. Mix until a soft dough forms, not too wet.

Turn out onto a floured surface, divide in two, and shape into an oval, about 2 inches in height.

Place in floured bread pans or on a floured baking sheet. Cut into each loaf however you want.

Bake 450 15-20 minutes, then reduce to 400 for another 20-25 minutes until bottom sounds hollow when tapped.
