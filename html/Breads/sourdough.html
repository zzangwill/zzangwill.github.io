Sourdough Bread
Recipe from: Cheeseboard(?)
Serves: 1 large loaf

Leaven:

  • 100g water
  • 50g whole wheat flour
  • 50g white bread flour
  • 1 tbs starter

Bread:

  • 100g leaven
  • 350g water
  • 450g white bread flour
  • 50g whole wheat flour
  • 10g salt, 25g water

Night before making dough, make leavening. Cover and let sit at room temp overnight. Next day, check if ready with a float test.

Mix leaven, 350g water, and bread flours. Stir until just incorporated, let sit for 20min.

Mix in remaining salt and water, let sit 30 min. Begin a series of folds every 30min, 4-6 times (2-3 hours).

Shape dough into boule and invert into heavily floured banneton. Cover with wrap and chill overnight.

Next day, preheat oven to 450 with dutch oven. Dump in dough cold, cook 25 minutes covered, 20-25 minutes uncovered. Check if bottom sounds hollow when tapped.
