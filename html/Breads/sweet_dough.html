Basic Sweet Dough
Recipe from: Stefan
Makes: 1 sweet dough serving (about 12 buns or rolls)
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk, or milk + 2 tsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • 6 tbsp butter, melted and cooled
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 1/4 cups flour (510g)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp yeast
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt

Whisk together buttermilk, butter, and eggs.

Combine 4 cups flour, sugar,yeast, and salt. (If using active dry yeast, let it activate in a little bit of water)

Mix wet ingredients into dry until dough comes together. You can do this in a stand mixer, or by hand.

Knead until dough is smooth and elastic. This is about 10 minutes on medium speed in a stand mixer, or by hand.

Form the dough into a ball and let it rise until doubled (usually about 2 hours).

Use the dough to form your rolls, or whatever baked good you are creating. Allow it a second rise and back at around 350F.
