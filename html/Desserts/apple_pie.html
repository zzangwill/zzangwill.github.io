Apple Pie
Recipe from: Stefan
Serves: 6-8
  • 2 pie crusts
  • 5-7 green apples
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup + 1 tb sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp lemon zest
  • 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 egg white

Roll 1 pie crust on 9-inch pan. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate 30 min.

Toss apples, 1/2 sugar, brown sugar, zest, salt, and cinnamon. Transfer to Dutch Ovven, cover and cook on medium heat, stirring frequently until apples are tender (around 15 min). Transfer to sheet with juive and let cool.

Oven rack on lowest position, heat to 425 with foil bake sheet under. Drain apples, keeping 1/4c of juice, mix with lemon juice.

Put apples in pan, mound in middle, pour in juices. Roll crust over, trim, and crimp. Add vents and brush with an egg white, sprinkle on sugar.

Bake 25 min until golden brown, reduce heat to 375 and bake 25-30 min more until bubbles and deep golden brown.
