Buttercream Frosting
Recipe from: Mother
Servings: 1 cake?
  • 1 lb powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/3 cup (2/3 stick) butter/margarine

In a mixing bowl or standing mixer, combine the powdered sugar, salt, milk, vanilla, and margarine/butter.

Beat in the mixer or with spoon until the batter is smooth and creamy, scraping the sides of the bowl often.

Make it to your taste by adding more butter, sugar, salt, etc! Use food dye to make it fun and colorful when decorating.
