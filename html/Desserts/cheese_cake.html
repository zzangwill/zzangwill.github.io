Cheesecake
Recipe from: Auntie Norma (Heather's Mother)
Serves: 10
  • 2 cups graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 tbs icing sugar
  • 1 1/2 lbs cream cheese (3 blocks)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp almond extract

Mix cracker crumbs, icing sugar, and melted butter.

Press above into spring form pan.

Mix cream cheese, eggs, sugar, and extract in thirds.

Bake at 315 for 45 minutes. Turn off oven, open door, and let cool inside the oven.

Refrigerate for 24 hours.
