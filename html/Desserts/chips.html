Chocolate Chip Cookies (super-secret coveted family recipe)
Recipe from: Family
Makes: 24 Cookies
  • 1 cup (2 stick) butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Beat butter or 1/2 tub margarine with sugars until smooth.

Add eggs, beat until creamy, then add vanilla.

Mix dry ingredients seperately. Add little by little to wet ingreidents.

In ~1 tbs amounts, put balls of dough on baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake at 375 for 9 minutes.
