Chocolate Cake
Recipe from: Grandma Phyllis
Serves: 6-8, or 1 Zepora
  • 1 Duncan Hines Chocolate Cake Mix
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 instant chocolate chip pudding
  • 1/2 cup chocolate syrup
  • 9oz chocoalte chips

Mix the Duncan Hines mix, sour cream, oil, warm water, eggs, chocolate pudding, and chocolate syrup together.

Gently add the chocolate chips.

Cook at 325 degrees Farenheit for 50-60 minutes. I suggest pulling it out of the oven when it still slightly jiggles, as it will cook in the pan when it cools!

Let the cake cool until comes off the walls of bundt pan, then remove from the pan by flipping it onto a plate. Sprinkle with powdered sugar, and serve (suggested serving with ice cream, preferabbly vanilla bean!).
