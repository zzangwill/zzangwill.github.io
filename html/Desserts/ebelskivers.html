Ebelskivers
Recipe from: Williams Sonoma
Makes: ~30

Batter:

  • 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 salt
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 1/3 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 5 egg whites
  • 5 tbsp unslated butter, melted for the pan
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Filling:

  • Any jam/preserve you think is delicious

For the batter, in a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, granulated sugar, and salt. In another bowl, lightly whisk the egg yolks, then whisk in the buttermilk and sour cream. Whisk the egg yolk mixture into the flour mixture until welel combined; the batter will be lumpy. Set aside. Beat the egg whites on high speed until stiff but not dry peaks form, around 2-3 minutes. Gently stir the whites into the batter in two additions.

Brush the ebelskiver cavities with melted butter. Heat the pan on medium. Pour 1 tbsp batter into each well and cook for 1 minute. Spoon 1/2 tsp filling into each center and top with more batter. Cook until the bottoms are golden brown and crispy, about 2 minutes more. Using a wooden skewer, flip the pancakes over and cook until golden brown and crispy, about 3 minutes more. Repeat with the remaining batter. In a small bowl, combine the confectioner's sugar and cinnamon anbd sprinke them on the ebelskivers.
