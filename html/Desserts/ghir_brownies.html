Ghirardelli Brownies
Recipe from: Ghirardelli
Serves: 16-20 Brownies
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup of butter or margarine, melted
  • 3/4 cup Ghirardelli sweet ground chocolate and cocoa
  • 2/3 cup sifted flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.

Stir the eggs with sugar and vanilla. Add the butter.

Sift the ground chocolate with flour, baking powder, and salt in another bowl. Stir this into the egg mixture.

Stir in the chips. Spread into a greased 8-9 inch square pan, baking for 20-30 minutes. Hypothetically, let it cool. Realistically, eat right away.
