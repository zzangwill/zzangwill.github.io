Hamentashen
Recipe from: Baba Diana
Serves: lots
  • 3/4 cup oil
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 cups flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Juice of 1 orange (1/4c)
  • filling (buy, or see recipe)

Beat the oil and sugar together. Add the eggs and vanilla, then orange juice. Seperately, mix the baking powder and flour then add this to the wet mixture.

Refridgerate to harden the dough. Do not roll it out too thin or make the cut outs too small! Bake at 350 degrees for 11-20 minutes until light brown.
