Peach Berry Crumble
Recipe from: Interwebs
Serves: 10
  • 1 cup quick oats
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup slivered almonds
  • 8 tbs (stick) unsalt butter, melt
  • 1 1/2 lb peaches (4-5)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup blubs, rasps
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • pinch of salt

To make the topping: combine oats, flour, brown sugar, salt, and cinnamon. Stir in butter the until gets crumbly, then toss in the almonds.

To make the filling: Bring pot to boil with bucket of ice ready. Cut an 'X' into bottom of the peaches. Blanche each peach for ~5 minutes until it gets wrinkly. Ice to cool them and remove their skin. (Honestly, I don’t mind the skin, so this step is skippable.) Slice the peaches.

In a large bowl, toss the peaches with sugar, flour, and salt. Fold in the berries. Let the bowl stand for 5 minutes.

Transfer the filling to buttered baking dish. Sprinkle it with the topping. Place the dish on baking sheet to capture any overflowing juices, and bake at 350 for around 50-55 minutes, when the dessert gets all bubbly and golden brown.
