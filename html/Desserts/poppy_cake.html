Poppy Seed Cake
Recipe from: Aunty Stacy
Makes: 1 Cake

Cake:

  • 2 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 3 tbs poppy seeds
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 cup flour

Glaze:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup OJ
  • 1/4 tsp almond extract

Grease the bundt pan. Beat the sugar, oil, eggs, vanilla, and almond extract together. Mix the flour, poppy seeds, baking powder, and salt seperately.

Stir in dry ingredients, alternating with mil (begin and end with the dry). Pour into a pan, and bake for 50 minutes at 350 degrees. Flip the cake, poke with a fork, and glaze it!
