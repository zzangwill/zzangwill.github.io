Respberry Lemon Squares
Recipe from: Baking DeCal
Serves: ~10
Source

The base:

  • 1/4 cup (50 grams) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest
  • 1/8 teaspoon table salt
  • 1/2 cup (4 ounces or 115 grams) unsalted butter, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup (125 grams) flour

Raspberry-Lemon Layer:

  • 1 cup (about 5 ounces or 140 grams) raspberries
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup (150 grams) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice; this should only require 2 lemons
  • 1/3 cup (40 grams) all-purpose flour
  • Confectioners sugar, for dusting

Preheat oven to 350. Line a 8x8x2 pan with parchment, letting it extend up two sides. Butter/spray the bottom.

For the base, mix sugar, zest, and salt. Then add butter and flour, mix until just combined and crumbly. Press dough into pan, 1/2 inch up sides. Bake 15 minutes until lighly browned edges, remove and cool (keep oven on).

For filling (while base cools), liquefy raspberries to get 1/3 cup puree. Whisk eggs, sugar, and lemon juice until smooth, and whisk in 3T (or more) of raspberry puree. Stir in flour, pour into cooling crust.

Bake 25-30 min, until barely jiggles and golden brown at the edges. Cool before cutting.
