Rum Cake
Recipe from: Mum!!
Makes: 1 Cake

Cake:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup white rum
  • 1 yellow cake mix
  • 1 instant vanilla pudding (small)

Glaze:

  • 1 stick margarine
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup white rum
  • 1/4 cup water

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Pour the cake mix and pudding mixture together. Beat the eggs, water, white rum, and oil together. Slowly add this to the yellow cake and instant pudding mixture.

Bake for 50 minutes in a pan with sprayed oil (ie bundt pan). Remove from the oven and poke several times. For the glaze: bring the ingredients to a boil in a pot for 1 minute. Pour the glaze over, and let it stand for 20 minutes. Then remove it from the pan.
