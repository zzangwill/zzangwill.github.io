Snikerdoodles
Recipe from: Baking DeCal
Makes: 20 Cookies
  • 2 3/4 cup flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 2 sticks (2tbs) unsalted butter at room temperature
  • 1 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbs cinnamon
  • 2 eggs

Sift dry ingredients together. Combine butter, 1.5 cup of sugar, and beat until light and fluffly. Add eggs and beat to combine.

Add dry ingredients, mix them until combined. Chill for 1 hour, then combing remaing 1/4 cup of sugar and cinnoman in a small bowl.

Scoop dough to form balls and roll them in cinnoman-sugar mix. Place ~2 inch apart on baking sheets. Bake for about 10 minutes at 400 degrees (at 5 min, rotate the sheet) until the cookies start to crack. They will NOT brown. Cool for 5 minutes on the rack.
