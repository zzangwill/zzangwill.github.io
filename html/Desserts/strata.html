Caramel Strata
Recipe from: Vicky's Daughter
Serves: 10
  • 1 cup packed dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tbs light corn syrup
  • Pecans, if desired
  • 12 bread slices (white or french loaf)
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Cinnamon and nutmeg

Combine sugar, butter, and syrup in a saucepan. Stir over low heat until butter melts and sugar dissolves. Bring it to a boil and pour into a 13x9x2 inch glass baking dish, tilting to coat the bottom evenly. Let it cool.

Arrange the bread slices in a single layer on the bottom, fitting snugly. Trim the bread to fit, and cover with a second layer of bread.

Whisk the milk, eggs, vanilla, and salt together. Pour this over the bread, top it off with cinnamon and nutmeg. Cover and chill overnight.

Bake at 350 degrees, for about 40 minutes until the bread is puffed and golden-brown. Serve with berries, whipped cream, and powdered sugar.
