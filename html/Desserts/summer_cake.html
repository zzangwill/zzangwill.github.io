Berry Lemon Summer Cake
Recipe from: Sandra Kavital
Serves: ~6
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g sugar or brown sugar
  • 80g cooking soy cream (liquid)
  • 20g neutral oil
  • 1/2 tsp fine salt
  • 150g sifted flour
  • 50g ground almonds
  • 2 tsp baking powder (9g)
  • 1 lemon zest
  • 100g milk
  • 1 tsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 300g of berries

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Line a square or springform pan with parchment paper or lightly butter/flour/cover the bottom only with the baking paper. Wash the fruits and drain them well.

In a bowl, whisk the eggs with the sugar or brown sugar until the mixture becomes pale and thickens a little. Add the cream, oil, and salt. Whisk to homogenize.

Mix the flour with the ground almonds and the baking powder. Gradually add to the bowl, stirring as you go so as not to form lumps and just enough to that the dough is smooth.

Add the finely grated lemon zest, milk, lemon juice, and vanilla extract and mix to obtain a smooth paste.

Pour into the mold and then scatter the fruit harmoniously on the surface of the cake.

Bake and cook for about 30 minutes then lower the thermostat to 300 degrees and continue to bake for 10-15 minutes more. Check the bake by pricking in the center with a toothpick, it should come out clean. Let it cool in the mold. Serve with icing sugar sprinkled on top.
