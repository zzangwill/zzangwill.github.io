Guacamole
Recipe from: Mr. Shah
Serves: yummy in my tummy
  • 1 cup avocado (1.39 mol)
  • 1/4 cup tomato (2 mol)
  • 1/4 cup onion (0.95 mol)
  • 1/4 cup mango (0.46 mol)
  • 3 tbs lime juice (0.55 mol)
  • Serrano - a pinch (0.01 mol)
  • 1/3 tsp salt (0.00475 mol)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper (0.028 mol)

Conversions:

  • Avocao - 115 g/mol
  • Onion - 42 g/mol
  • Lime - 18 g/mol
  • Serrano - 13 g/mol
  • Tomato - 20 g/mol
  • Mango - 87 g/mol
  • Black Pepper - 10 g/mol
  • Salt - NaCl
  • 160 g = 1 cup
  • 1 tbs = 3.3g
  • 1 tsp 1.1g
