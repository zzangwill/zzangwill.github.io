Rum Cake
Recipe from: Stacy
Serves: ~10

Meatballs:

  • 5 lb ground beef
  • 3 tbs soy sauce
  • 4 tsp garlic powder
  • ~3 tbs ketchup

Sauce:

  • 3 large onions, diced
  • 3 tbs brown sugar
  • Almost two 14oz ketchup bottles
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon

Mix the beef with soy sauce, garlic, and the ketchup. Roll the mixture into small balls and set them aside.

Mix the onions, brown sugar, ketchup, and lemon juice into a large pot. Bring it all to a boil, then add the meatballs one at a time, so they do not touch. Reduce the heat tolow, and cook for 1 hour or more.
