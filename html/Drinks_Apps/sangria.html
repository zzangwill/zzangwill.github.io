Sangria
Recipe from: Me and Mom
Serves: ~6-8
  • 2 bottles of red wine
  • 1 cup of brandy, OJ, and pomegranate juice
  • 1/2 cup of triple sec or contreiu
  • 1/2 cup agave
  • Plus, any fruit you want. Recommended: apple slices, orange slices, nectarines, peaches, blackberries, blueberries, raspberries. What's seasonal?

Mix it all together! That's it. :) Best to let it sit for a while before serving.
