Spinach Gomaae
Recipe from: i<3Umami
Makes: 3
  • sea salt
  • 1 large bundle of fresh spinach
  • 1/5 tbs of tahini
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 smal clove of garlic, grated
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil

Boil a pot of water, and season it with a pinch of salt.

In the meantime, prepare the sesame sauce. Mix the sauce ingredients with 1.5 tbs of water. Rinse and add the spinach to quickly blanche them, about 10-15 seconds. Drain and rinse the spinach under cold water.

Squeeze the greens gently to remove excess water. Place in a serving bowl and drizzle the sauce over; toss the greens. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Note: can microwave frozen spinach. You will still need to squeeze out the water.
