Banana Curry Chicken
Recipe from: Mom
Serves: how ever many it takes to eat 1 whole chicken
  • 1 portioned chicken
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 tsp curry powder
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbs honey
  • 1 tsp mustard (dijon is best)
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chutney
  • 3 or 4 bananas

Rub chicken with oil. Combine the salt, curry, cinnamon, and rub this on the chicken. Let it stand for about 15 minutes.

Combine the honey, lemon juice, mustard, and chutney. Pour it over the chicken, making sure it is well-coated. Marinate for 1 hour minumum, up to 24 hours. Overnight is typically best.

Bake covered at 375F for 1.5 hours. Remove from oven, and placed sliced bananas length-wise on chicken and cook uncovered for another half hour.
