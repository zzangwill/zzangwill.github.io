Stuffed Peppers
Recipe from: Mom
Makes: 6 servings

Sauce:

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup beef/bullion broth

Stuffing:

  • 1 onion
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • Veggie of choice (ie diced shrooms)
  • 1 lb ground meat
  • Lots of thyme
  • 3 bell peppers, halved with seeds removed

Mix the sauce ingredients together and let them simmer to taste in a pan. Saute onion and garlic. Add veggie of choice and saute, then put aside.

Clean and halve green/red peppers, removing the seeds and keeping the top so it's like a boat. Stuff with meat mixture and place in a baking dish.

Add sauce to the dish and spoon some on top of the peppers. Cover with parchment paper and tightly seal with joul. Bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour. Remove the foil, and spoon more sauce on top. Bake uncovered for 20-30 more minutes.
