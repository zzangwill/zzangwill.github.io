Thai Chiken Strips
Recipe from: Mum!
Serves: 3-4

We prefer to use boneless/skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips.

Marinate:

  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Peanut butter
  • Fish sauce
  • Thai chili paste
  • Sesame oil
  • Olive oil

Coating:

  • Chopped pecans/peanuts/bread crumbs - your own ratio, to taste!

Marinate the chicken strips in fridge. Remove them around 20-30 minutes before you want to eat and roll them around in the coating/crumb mixture.

Preheat baking sheet with foil at 425. Cook 10 minutes on one side, then flip and cook for 5 more minutes.

Serve with a veggie, some fried rice, and sweet chili sauce.
