Creamy Curry Udon
Recipe from: Farahjeats
Serves: 2
  • 2 servings udon noodles
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 5 mushrooms sliced
  • Green onion for garnishing
  • 2 tbsp butter, or 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 cubes Japanese curry
  • 1 cup cooking cream or evaporated milk, whipping cream (regular milk OK too)
  • Chili flakes/oil (optional)

Boil the udon noodles seperately and drain.

Slice the mushrooms and mine the garlic.

Add butter or oil to a pan/wok on medium-low heat. Add the garlic and saute until it becomes aromatic, then add the mushrooms and continue sauteing. Add the dairy product, and once it begins to simmmer, add the curry cubes. Stir through until the curry cubes melt and mixes in. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a splash of water.

Add the noodles and mix until every strand is coated in the sauce. Serve it on a plate, and drizzle chili oil and garnish with green onions.
