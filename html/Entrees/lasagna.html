Lasagna
Recipe from: Aunty Stacy
Makes: 6
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 16oz bag of spinash, frozen
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 15oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  • 5 cup tomato sauce
  • Lasagna noodles

Saute the onions in olive oil and garlic. Defrost the spinash and make sure to squeeze out the excess water. Add the spinash to the onion mixture, and saute for another minute or two. When it is all mixed in, add the cottage cheese and ricotta cheese to the onion mixture.

Place 1/2 to 1 cup of tomato sauce in a 9x11 pan and add 1/4 cup of water. Put a layer of noodles on top of the sauce, then layer with the cheese/onion/spinash mixture on top of the noodles. Cover with mozzarella cheese (1/3 of a cup) and 1/3 cup of parmesan. Repeat sauce, noodles, onion mix, and cheeses. On the top you can put another layer of noodles, sauce, and mozzarella/parmesan.

Cover with aluminium foil and bake in a pre-heated oven at 350 degrees for 50 minutes. Uncover and cook for another 10 minutes.
