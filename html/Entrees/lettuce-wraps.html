Turkey Lettuce Wraps
Recipe from: PF Chang's
Serves: 4
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1lb ground turkey
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup hoison sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 1 tsp Sriracha, or more, to taste
  • 1 (8oz) can whole water chestnuts, drained and diced
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 head of butter lettuce

Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the ground turkey and cook until browned, about 3-5 minutes making sure to crumble the turkey as it cooks.

Stir in garlic, onion, hoison sauce, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, ginger, and Sriracha until onions have beecome translucent, about 1-2 minutes. Stir in chestnuts and green onions until tender, aout 1-2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.

To serve, spoon several tablespoons of the turkey mixture into the center of a lettuce leaf, taco-syle.
