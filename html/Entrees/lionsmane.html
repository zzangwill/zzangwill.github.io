Lion's Mane Cream of Mushroom Soup
Recipe from: Medical Enthobotany
Serves: 4
  • 3 cups Lion's Mane, diced
  • 1 cup scallions, chopped
  • 3 tbsp butter (unsalted)
  • 1 clove fresh garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
  • 1 cup of whole milk + 1 cup heavy cream (or 2 cups half and half)
  • 2 tbsp Sherry
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Some sliced scallions for garnish

Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat. Add 1 cup of scallions and stir often (do not burn it), sautee for 2-3 minutes. Add mushroms, garlic, and cook for another 3 minutes. Then sprinkle in the flour and cook for 3 minutes more, while chopping/mixing and stirring to keep it from clumping too much. Add the chicken stock and whisk like hell to seperate the clumps.

Bring the pot to a low boil, then durn it down to a low simmer for 30 minutes, covered with a lid. After the 30 minutes, add hte milk, cream, salt, and pepper while stirring. Serve with oyster crackers and garnish with scallions!
