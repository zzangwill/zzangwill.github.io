Mac 'n' Cheese
Recipe from: Barbara Grace (Daryl's Mom)
Serves: ~10
  • 1 tbs light olive oil
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 1/2 bag vermicelli noodles
  • 1/2 tsp salt + pepper
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Dash cayenne pepper
  • 6 cup water
  • 2 cup mild cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup Montery Jack

In a medium pot, boil water with olive oil and a dash of salt. Add noodles, boil 10-12 min (no longer). Drain noodles.

Spray baking dish, poar in noodles. Add butter while noodles are hot. Add rest of pepper, salt, cayenne, mix with spoon while noodles are hot. Fold in jack cheese and 3/4 of cheddar cheese.

In another large bowl, beat eggs adding milk as you do so. Sprinkle rest of cheddar evenly over the top and sprinkle paprika.

Lastly, pour eggs/milk mixture over -- it should slightly cover. Add more milk if not enough.

Bake 45-55 min at 350 until golden brown. Any longer, it will dry out too much.
