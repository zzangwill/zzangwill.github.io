Meatsauce
Recipe from: Me and Mom
Serves: 2
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • Basil and crushed oregano
  • Tomato paste
  • Chopped onion
  • Zucchini, grated
  • Garlic, minced
  • Wine

Saute the garlic and onion. Add the herbs, crushing the oregano. Add the meat, and mash it thouroughly.

Add tomato paste, mix it together. Grate the zucchini, making sure to squeeze out the excess water. Add the zucchini.

Pour in the tomato sauce. Rinse out the jar with wine a few times, adding the wine into the sauce.

Cook for as long as possible, on a low simmer.

Can freeze to transport or eat at a later date. Best served with delicious salad and fresh bread.
