Paella
Recipe from: A Youtube Video Mom and I Watched
Serves: 3-4
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Meat of choice
  • 200g butter beans
  • 6 artichoke hearts
  • Chopped garlic
  • Heaping spoonful smoked paprika
  • One chopped tomato
  • 1.5L (50oz) meat stock
  • 1.2 gram crushed saffron
  • 250g rice (short grain)

Add salt and olive oil to pan. On high heat, cook meat untl dark brown caramelized color. Add garlic, beans, artichoke. Bring to heat and a-stir.

Add paprika, tomato, and stir. Turn heat back up and stir for 2 more minutes.

Add meat stock and meat, stir a bit and add safforn. Bring to boil and add rice. Stir, and let rice spread evenly. Bring back to boil and stir one last time.

Let cook on highest heat for 10 minutes, low heat for 6 minutes more, NO MORE stirring.

Cover with foil, lower heat for 5 minutes, then let rest 5 minutes. Serve with lemon!
