Spinash Tomato Cheese Quiche
Recipe from: Baking DeCal
Serves: 6
  • 1 pie crust
  • 1/2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sundried tomato, drain, chop
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 cups packed spinach
  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled

Cook spinach with oil and garlic, S+P, until wilted.

Line pie dough in 9inch pie dish. Use pie weights or poke holes, bake at 350 for 8 minutes.

While crust is in the oven, whisk eggs and milk together until combined. Stir in tomatoes, cheese, and spinach. Add more salt and pepper.

Remove crust from oven and pour in egg mixture.

Bake until top is golden brown and center is no longer jiggly, 45-55 min. Allow to cool 5 minutes before serving.
