Saffron Chicken
Recipe from: Mother
Serves: 6-8
  • 3 1/2 lb chicken thighs and drumsticks
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and finely diced
  • 1/4 tsp crumbled saffron
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry sherry
  • 2 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 can (14.5oz) diced tomatoes with juices
  • 3 cups rice, coucous, or quinoa
  • 1/2 cup green olives, pitted and halved
  • 1 tbs minced fresh flat-leaf parsley

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Season chicken with salt and black pepper. In Dutch Oven over medium-high heat, warm olive oil. Brown the chicken in batches, 7-8min per batch. Transfer to plate, and discard the fat.

In the same pot, over medium heat, warm olive oil. Cook the onion, bell pepper, and saffron about 10 minutes. Add garlic; cook and add the sherry, cook for 1 minute more. Add the chicken, chicken broth, tomatoes, and juices. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat to medium-low, cover and cook for 20 minutes. Add the rice, olives, salt, and black pepper. Increase heat to medium-high and bring the pot to a simmer. Cover the pot back in an oven for 30 minutes, stirring once halfway through.

Let stand 10 minutes. Enjoy!
