Maple-Baked Salmon
Recipe from: Genevieve Ko
Serves: 4
  • 1 (1/5 pound) salmon filet
  • 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons whole-grain Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoon mayo
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

Remove salmon from the refrigerator. Heat oven to 325 degrees.

Stir maple syrup, mustard, and mayo together until well mixed.

Season the salmon with salt and pepper and place in a baking dish, skin-side down (if with skin). Slather the maple sauce all over the top.

Bake until a knife slides into the center with only little resistance, 15-20 minutes. When you remove the knife and touch the blade with your upper lip, it should feel very warm but not hot. The salmon will continue to heat out of the oven while in the baking dish.
