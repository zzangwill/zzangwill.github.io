Scallion Meatballs with a Soy-Ginger Glaze
Recipe from: Mom
Serves: 4

Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup mirin rice wine
  • 1/4 cup ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 4 whole black peppercorns

Meatballs:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 4-6 chopped scallions
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 tbs sesame oil
  • Ground black pepper

Bring sugar to boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat, stirring until the sugar melts completely. Reduce the heat to medium-low, add soy sauce, mirin, ginger, coriander, and peppercorns. Simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 30 minutes. Strain through a sieve.

Mix the turkey, scallions, egg, sesame oil, soy sause, and pepper in a bowl. Roll tablespoons of the mixture into balls. Cook them in a skillet with oil over medium-high heat. Turn them until they are browned, about 8 minutes per batch. Spoon the sauce over.

You can keep the meatballs warm in an oven at 200 degrees. Garnish with scallions.
