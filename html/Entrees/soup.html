Mushroom Soup
Recipe from: Aunty Stacy
Serves: 4
  • 6 tbs margarine, melted
  • 1 onion, leek, celery stalk, and potato
  • 4 cups soup stalk
  • 2 tbs mushrooms
  • can of puree if desired
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Melt margarine and add leek, celery, and potato.

Cover and cook 15 min, stirring occasionally.

Add mushrooms, cook for 10 minutes.

Add the stock and puree if desired, s+p to taste.
