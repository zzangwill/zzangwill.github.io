Mom's Famous Stuffing
Recipe from: Can you take a guess?
Serves: 6-8
  • 3 packages of crackers (unsalted)
  • 1/2 package of Corn Flakes
  • 2-3 onions
  • 2-3 sticks of margarine
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 3 eggs
  • Some celery

Saute the onions and celery in margarine, let them cool. Mix crushed Corn Flakes and crackers, and add poultry seasoning. Pour the veggies over the dry ingredients. Beat and add the eggs, then add the warm broth. Mix it well and season to taste. Bake at 350 degrees until golden-brown, for about 30-40 minutes.
