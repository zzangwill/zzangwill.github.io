Tilapia Vera Cruz
Recipe from: Mom
Serves: 4
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbs oregano
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Tomatoes - fresh or canned, 2 fresh or 1 small can
  • 2 tbs capers
  • 3-4 fish fillets
  • 1/2 cup green olives
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Brown fish in a pan with olive oil, seasoned lightly Take the fish out, and add garlic and oniojns. Saute, then add oregano and stir. Add the capers, bell peppers, and saute. Add the tomatoes and cook down for about 30 minutes until saucey. Add fish back in, add the olives, and a squeeze of lime. Simmer for about 20 minutes.
