Tomato Egg Drop Soup
Recipe from: Mom
Serves: 4
  • 3 bulbs of green onion, chopped
  • 8 oz ripe tomatoes (~2 medium sized), chopped finely
  • 1 tbs oil
  • 2 large eggs, whisked
  • 2-2 1/4 cup chicken bone broth
  • Toasted sesame oil drizzle
  • Coarse sea salt, to taste

Chop the green onion and tomatoes, set them aside. Add oil to a medium pot, saute the whites of the onion, then add the tomatoes and salt for 2-3 minutes until the tomatoes are soft. Add the broth and simmer for 8-10 minutes.

In the meantime, whisk the eggs until they are foamy. Seaon the soup to taste (recommend using mushroom umami flavor). Bring the soup to a hot, gentle boil/simmer. Slowly drizzle the eggs from high up, and with the other hand quickly stir the soup.

Off the heat, add the sesame oil and green onion.
