Turkey Meatloaf (aka Turkeyloaf, NOT Tonyloaf)
Recipe from: Mum
Serves: 5
  • 2 tbs margarine
  • 1 cup onion, minced
  • 3 gloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/4 lb ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup of ketchup
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Melt the margatine in a skillet, cook onion and garlic in the margarine (or olive oil) for five minutes. Place mixture into a large bowl and let it cool for five minutes.

Combine the turkey, bread, crumbs, egg, and 1/4 crup of the ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and peper with the onion mixture. Press it into a loaf pan or shape into a loaf on a baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 50-55 minutes (less on cookie sheet). The internal temperature should reach 165 degrees.
