Tuscan Tuna Salad Bowl
Recipe from: Ian's Cookbook
Serves: 1
  • 1 small shallot, sliced thin
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 3/4 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, plus 1 1/2 tablesppon sun-dried tomato oil
  • 1 (5oz) can solid white tuna in water, drained and flaked
  • 1/4 head Bibb lettuce torn into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/4 cup canned cannellini beans, rinsed
  • 1 celery rib, sliced thin on bias, plus 2 tablespoons celery leaves

Whisk shallot, garlic, mustard, lemon juice, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. While whisking constantly, slowly drizzle in sun-dried tomato oil until combined. Set aside for 5 minutes.

Toss sun-dried tomatoes and tuna with 1/2 tablespoon vinaigrette to coat. In seperate bowl, toss lettuce with 1/2 tabelspoon vinaigrette to coat, then season with salt and pepper to taste. Top with tuna mixture, beans, and sliced celery. Drizzle with remaining 1 tablespoon vinaigrette and sprinkle with celery leaves. Serve.
