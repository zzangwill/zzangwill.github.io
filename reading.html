Everything Is Tuberculosis: The History and Persistence of Our Deadliest Infection by John Green
My rating: 5 of 5 stars

Such a great audiobook to listen to, as John Green really gets across what he wants to emphatically emphasize, or tell softly to the reader as he describes horrific end results of a global health crisis. However, is really everything tuberculosis? Not in this book, honestly: Green spends a lot of time describing the human condition and allocation of resources that devalues the lives of those most greatly affected by this disease, instead of focusing on TB. This is, however, probably the key message he is trying to get across: we MUST change our priorities, and we MUST understand that we have the means to lift everyone, but are choosing not to. We CAN do better.
